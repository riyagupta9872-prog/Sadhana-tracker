📖 How to Use Sadhana Tracker

🏠 What is this App?

📿This app helps you track your daily spiritual practices (Sadhana) — like chanting, reading, hearing, and service. Your coordinator can see your progress and guide you better.

📝 How to Fill Daily Entry

1️⃣Tap Daily Entry tab at the top.
2️⃣Select today's date from the dropdown.
3️⃣Fill in what time you slept last night, woke up, and completed chanting.
4️⃣Enter minutes spent in Reading, Hearing, and Service.
5️⃣Tap Submit Sadhana. Done! ✅
⚠️You can only submit once per day. Fill it every day for best results.

📊 Scoring System — How are marks given?

Each day maximum score is 160 points. Each week maximum is 1120 points.

📈 My Reports

📅Shows your last 4 weeks. Each week shows daily scores, totals, and percentage.
💚Green % = good week. Orange = needs improvement. Red = missed many days.
📥Download your full history as Excel file anytime.

📉 My Progress

📊See your score trend as a chart — Daily, Weekly, or Monthly view.

🔔 Notifications

🔔Tap the bell in the menu → Allow → You will get reminders if you forget to fill Sadhana for 2+ days.

❓ Common Questions

QCan I fill yesterday's entry? Yes, select yesterday's date from the dropdown.
QI made a mistake. Can I edit? Only your coordinator/admin can edit submitted entries.
QWhat if I forget for many days? Those days show as NR (Not Reported) with -35 penalty each.
